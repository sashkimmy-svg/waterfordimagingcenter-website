SERVICES

Ultrasound

is a non-invasive, radiation-free medical imaging technique that uses high-frequency sound waves to
create real-time images of body's internal organs, tissues and blood flow

X-RAY

is a fast, painless imaging test that uses a small amount
of electromagnetic radiation to produce the images of structures inside the body, particularly bones

ELECTROCARDIOGRAM ECG

is a quick, safe and painless test that records
the electrical signals of your heart to chec for various heart conditions.
